Our story

Founded in 2020, Pancake Pastries was born from a love of buttery layers, seasonal fruit & morning light. We focus on small batches, quality ingredients, and sustainable sourcing.

Our philosophy

We celebrate craft baking: laminated doughs, slow-fermented doughs, real butter, house-made custards and hand-cut fruit. We bake for flavour, not speed.

Sustainability & community

We partner with local farmers for seasonal fruit and minimize food waste by making small batches and offering next-day specials to staff and neighbours.
